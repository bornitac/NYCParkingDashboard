# NYC Parking Complaints & Traffic Analysis

An interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing NYC parking complaints, traffic incidents, and parking meter data to identify patterns across all five boroughs. Built using merged public datasets from NYC Open Data.

🔗 **Live Dashboard:** https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/bornita.chowdhury/viz/NYCParkingComplaintsDashboard

---

## 🔍 What It Does

- Analyzes parking complaint density, resolution times, and safety issues by borough
- Merges NYC 311 complaints, traffic incident, and parking meter datasets
- Identifies patterns in complaint frequency and resolution across New York City
- Delivers actionable insights to support city planning and resource allocation decisions

---

## 📊 Dashboards

The workbook contains 6 interactive dashboards covering:
- Parking complaint density by borough
- Complaint resolution times
- Traffic safety incidents
- Parking meter locations and status
- Borough-level trend comparisons
- Resource allocation insights
