# NYC Parking Complaints & Traffic Analysis

An interactive Tableau Story analyzing NYC parking complaints, traffic incidents, parking infrastructure, and resident satisfaction across all five boroughs. Using public datasets from NYC Open Data and Kaggle, this project identifies patterns in parking demand, safety concerns, and public perceptions of city services.

**Live Dashboard:**
https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/bornita.chowdhury/viz/NYCParkingAnalysis_17806063742630/NYCParkingAnalysis#1

--- 

## What It Does
This analysis combines NYC parking complaint, traffic accident, parking meter, and resident satisfaction data to explore key transportation and parking challenges throughout New York City.

Key questions explored include:

Which boroughs experience the highest volume of parking complaints?
How does parking meter availability compare across boroughs?
How have parking complaints changed over time?
What are the most common causes of traffic accidents?
How satisfied are residents with 311 complaint responses?

---
Story Sections
1. Complaint Distribution & Parking Availability
This section explores where parking complaints are most concentrated across NYC boroughs and compares complaint volume with parking meter availability. The analysis highlights areas that might have higher greater parking challenges.

2. Trends & Causes
This section examines how parking complaints have changed over time and identifies the leading causes of traffic accidents. Together, these trends provide insights into transportation and parking-related issues across the city.

4. Infrastructure & Satisfaction
This section analyzes parking meter distribution and resident satisfaction with 311 complaint responses. The findings reveal differences in parking infrastructure and public perceptions across the boroughs.
